About LMGTFY

LMGTFY is for all those people who find it more convenient to bother you with their question rather than google it for themselves.

It was inspired during a lunch conversation between @coderifous and his friends: @tmassing, @rmm5t, @EricStratton, and @methodvon.

About LMGTFY2

LMGTFY2 is an archived version of LMGTFY from the year 2018.

The restoration was done in response to the original being offline by m8tec and the help of kavemans.dev.

All credits go to the original LMGTFY - you guys made a great job!
